About Us



Pronounced /'rāvən'/, we are a rescue animal volunter network dedicated to transporting our furry friends to their new, forever home. If you would like to volunteer your time to help on a leg of one of our animal 's journey, please sign up and we' ll be happy to get you set up to help a loving animal in need!



Our Mission



What started out as a mutual love for animals has grown to become a passion to link the animal rescue groups with the volunteers that help transport animals to a better life. Rather than relying on a blog posting, Tran will give all of the volunteers a way to both volunteer and track the animals to their destinations.
